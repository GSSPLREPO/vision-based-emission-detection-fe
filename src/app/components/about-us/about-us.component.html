📌 Product Brief

  • ✅ This product aims to automatically detect chimney gas emissions from CCTV or IP camera feeds.
  • 🔍 We use a custom-trained YOLOv12 model to detect chimneys in real-time video streams.
  • 🎨 After chimney detection, a region above it is scanned using OpenCV color detection to identify yellow-colored gases , which may indicate pollution or chemical emissions.
  • 📦 The detection results are tracked using SORT/DeepSORT algorithms to assign unique IDs to chimneys and follow them over time.
  • 💾 All detections and alerts are logged into an MSSQL database for reporting, auditing, and evidence storage.
  • 📡 The system supports real-time streaming using WebSocket-based communication between client and server.
  • 🧠 Calibration workflows help reconfigure detection zones if the camera moves or is disturbed . Users can manually adjust chimneys if needed.
  • 📲 In future releases, the system will send SMS alerts to authorities in case of yellow gas detection or camera tampering.
  • 🎯 Overall, we aim to build a scalable, real-time environmental monitoring solution that empowers industries and agencies to respond faster to pollution events.
